A twitter-like website. But instead of tweeting the users can howl.

Howler is a twitter-like website that is only available during the few days of the month when there is a full moon. Every moon cycle, all of the data that is collected is processed to give users insight into what the most popular topics and conversations were during that period, and then all data is deleted. This gives users the opportunity to get a glimpse into what matters to the world, without the need to sift through countless conversations. With Howler, users can quickly understand what matters most in the world and engage with the topics that matter.
